The beginning...


In the beginning, a long long time ago, the land of Eden was plagued by illness, terrifying monsters and unspeakable evil that all wreaked havoc upon mankind, all hope seemed lost
until a group of magical beings appeared. These beings went to war with all the monstrosities that almost destroyed the mortal realm and with a righteous right hand they conquered all evil.
Truly I tell you these powerful beings were the answer to all living things that cried out for peace, they were the incarnations of justice.
After the war they lead Eden into a new era, an era of peace, love and tranquility. They became legends and from that day onwards they would forever be known as The Guardians, immortal heroes of Eden.
The Guardians swore to forever watch over Eden and protect all that is good.
