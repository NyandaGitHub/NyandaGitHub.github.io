The beginning (Part 2)...


There was one Guardian, however, who began questioning the manner in which the mortal realm was being governed by the Guardians. His name was Parrallex.
Each of the 9 Guardians was in charge of guarding a certain aspect of humanity and Parrallex was the guardian of dreams.
With just a sprinkle of sand he could control and manipulate ones dreams.
Parrallex grew tired of the dream world, he wanted more. He was forever cursed to watch mortals live out their wildest and greatest dreams while simultaneously being denied his own.
Parrallex rebelled against the other Guardians for total control over the land of Eden...
