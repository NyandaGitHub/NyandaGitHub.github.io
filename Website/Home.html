Welcome to CHAPTERS dear reader!


Here at CHAPTERS we have a very special mode of entertainment ready for you, and they are, interactive stories! Here at CHAPTERS we will not just let you simply read our stories but we will let you decide the narrative of those stories by presenting you with options to choose from while you are reading which can change the narrative and course of the stories completely. Whats more? You can send us your own story via the Submission page and if approved we will turn it into an interactive story and upload it to this very site for everyone to enjoy. Navigate towards the Story page to begin going on adventures you will never forget!
