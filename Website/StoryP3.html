The beginning (Part 3)...


Parrallex lashed out against the other heroes and one by one they each fell. The guardians known as Triton, Demeter, Pothos, Artemus and Gaia all met their end.
Only three remained. Anansi, Horus and the most powerful of all, Kronos. As each guardian died the mortal realm grew more and more unstable and Parrallex grew more powerful.
It had reached the point where even if the three remaining legendary heroes joined forces, victory against the betrayer was very unlikely to occur.
The world needed a champion, thus a weapon was forged...
